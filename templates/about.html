About Trackdemic

Trackdemic is a simple academic tracking web application designed to help students log their daily learning activities, monitor progress, and view leaderboards to see who's on top.

The platform features:

  • Add or remove and view personal study logs
  • Track performance over time
  • Compare with classmates via leaderboard
  • Simple and Easy.

This project was developed to help students monitor their whole Academic Expierience.
